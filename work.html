Behind the Screens.

A glimpse into how these pieces come alive, evolving through sketches, revisions, and the quiet work that happens off to the side.

   I work with chaos, not straight lines.

Ideas show up out of order, half-formed, mostly when I'm nowhere near my desk. I remember their sparks. Twist them, pull them apart, sketch them badly, redo them, abandon them, then loop right back until the vision finally comes into focus. It's a back and forth between impulse, instinct, mood, frustration, tiny breakthroughs that suddenly paint a picture.
This is how I work. It's messy, but it's mine.
